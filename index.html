Crystals Collector!

Do you have what it takes to defeat the dragon and steal his riches?

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are also four weapons given; sword, shield, magic, and bow. By clicking on one of them, you will reveal the weapons' attack value, and add that specific amount of attack points to your total score.

Face the dragon, win the game, and steal its' riches by matching your total score with the random number you were given at the start of the game!

Don't forget...the amount of points that each attack choice gives you is hidden from you until you click it...Which attacks are effective now may not be as effective next game!
